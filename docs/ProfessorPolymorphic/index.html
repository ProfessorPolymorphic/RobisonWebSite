Musings at the Intersection of Horror and Science

Hello! This BLOG is where I share my thoughts on how science informs horror fiction, and whether horror fiction can be used to at least help teach science, if not directly inform scientific research. The posts that appear here will vary in their focus. Some will directly address the intersection of horror and science, while some might focus primarily on either horror fiction or science concepts.

My basic premise is driven by the Lovecraft quote you see at the top of the screen. I find this compelling because I am a scientist, and science is one of two tools humanity has invented that pushes back against the unknown.
